VEGAN SPAGHETTI SAUCE AND MEATBALLS
A delicius and iconic Italian dish best for any occasion!

Kitchen Tools



Direction
1. In a large bowl, combine the ground Beyond Meat, bread crumbs, parsley, Violife Vegan Parmeasan cheese, 1/4
teaspoon of black pepper, garlic powder and beaten egg. Mix well and form into 12 balls. Store, covered, in
refridgerator until needed.
2. In a large saucepan over medium heat, saute the onion and garlic in olive oil until the onion is translucent in color.
Stir in the tomatoes, salt and sugar. Cover, reduce the heat to low and simmer for 90 minutes. Stir in the
tomatoes, basil, 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper and meatballs. Simmer for 30 additional minutes. Serve.
